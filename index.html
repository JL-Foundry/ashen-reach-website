Know Your Enemies

✦

Dominion Eternal

// AMBER-IRON · 200 SYSTEMS

Order maintained through compliance fleets and psychological conditioning. They are not entirely wrong to call themselves civilisation's last guarantor.

"Order is the only kindness."

◈

Fractured Covenant

// BLOOD-RED · FREE SPACE

A coalition dissolved into warlords and idealists. The only currency that matters is reputation, and reputation is written in blood.

"Survive, or don't."

⬡

Vorath Hegemony

// TOXIC-GREEN · 90 SYSTEMS

Ancient deep-ocean predators. They do not conquer — they consume. Every species in their path is a resource to be processed.

"All things are resources."

◯

Hollow Church

// VOID-VIOLET · NO FIXED SPACE

Death cultists travelling in cathedral-ships. Their warriors remove their own pain receptors. They have been waiting for someone to find the cannon.

"Only the fire is honest."
